About PDFTEX

Transforming the way you work with PDFs and LaTeX

Our Mission

At PDFTEX, we're on a mission to simplify the process of working with PDF documents and LaTeX. Our primary goal is to make it easier for people with zero or low knowledge of LaTeX to edit, add, or use pre-made formulas and text, and integrate them into their PDF documents in an easy and, most importantly, free way. We understand that the majority of users who need these services are students, often with limited financial resources. That's why we're committed to providing a powerful, accessible alternative to expensive apps that offer similar functionality. We believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background or budget constraints. Our goal is to provide a seamless, user-friendly platform that bridges the gap between PDF and LaTeX, empowering researchers, students, and professionals to focus on their content rather than formatting challenges or software costs.

Our Story

The idea for PDFTEX was born from personal experience. As a math Ph.D. student, I found myself in a challenging situation while working on my master's thesis. My research focused on domain decomposition methods, a topic that was new to me and required significant time to understand, prepare codes, and prove convergence theorems. With the deadline looming and limited time remaining, I faced the daunting task of writing my thesis. My limited experience with LaTeX added another layer of complexity. I desperately needed a solution to extract LaTeX code from PDFs to incorporate into my thesis. Unfortunately, most available software options were paid services. After a few attempts, I reluctantly paid for these services to complete my work on time. This experience sparked a realization: there had to be a better, more accessible way to work with LaTeX and PDFs. That's when the idea for PDFTEX was born.
